## Methodology

- **Holdings**: Zerodha Kite Connect (live)
- **Fundamentals**: yfinance + Screener.in + Tickertape (cross-checked)
- **Analyst targets**: yfinance consensus aggregating 1-43 analysts/stock
- **News**: yfinance + Google News RSS (CNBC TV18, MoneyControl, ET, Business Today, etc.)
- **Technical**: RSI, MACD, SMA 20/50/200, EMA, Bollinger, ATR(14) on 365-day candles
- **30-day forecast**: ARIMA(p,d,q) auto-AIC over log close
- **12-month forecast**: Monte Carlo bootstrap of 252 daily log returns × 1000 sims
- **Buy upto** = min(consensus_target × 0.92, current × 1.05) — leave 8% margin to consensus, never chase >5% over LTP
- **Stop loss** = max(current - 2×ATR, 52-week-low × 1.05) — volatility-aware bail-out level

*This is research output, not financial advice. ML projections have wide uncertainty bands — treat them as scenarios, not forecasts.*
